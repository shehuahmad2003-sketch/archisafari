Trip Highlights

An Immersive Study of Architecture & Nature

Join us for a transformative five-day field excursion to Yankari Game Reserve. Explore the Wikki Warm Springs, Marshall Caves, and diverse savanna wildlife while studying sustainable eco-tourism structures and vernacular architecture of Bauchi.

01Study of vernacular and traditional architecture in Bauchi
02Analysis and documentation of eco-tourism facility planning
03Wildlife observation and on-site architectural sketching
04Marshall Caves exploration and geological site study
05Group critique sessions and evening presentations
